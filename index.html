The Dining Hall staff will prepare and serve:

Every day, with the exception of Wednesday dinner.

Notification and Payment

  • Email Kathy Piehl (dininghall@pinewoodscamp.org) to notify her of arrival and departure for planning, including the number of people and any dietary restrictions. This is essential for Kathy to order and prepare appropriate food quantities.
  • Billing sheets will be submitted by Wednesday lunch for any meals during the previous week. Bills will be sent to an email or mailing address of choice with payment due upon receipt.
  • Tips for the Dining Hall staff are welcome and are divided among staff at the end of the season. Tips can be left in the Tip Box or paid through the billing sheet. Any checks should be made out to "Pinewoods Association" with "Tip Fund" written as a memo.

COVID Protections

As of June 22, 2021 the Governor of Michigan has rescinded all executive orders related to COVID-19, therefore lifting the protective measures and requirements at the state level. The following guidelines are designed to help Pinewoods members, guests, and staff remain as safe as possible while using the Dining Hall this summer.

  • We encourage everyone using the Dining Hall to get vaccinated against COVID-19 if possible.
  • Signage on entry encourages people to self-screen, wear masks if unvaccinated, clean hands, and limit indoor socializing.
  • Diners are asked to stay home if any of the following is true: experiencing COVID-19 symptoms (fever, cough, shortness of breath, loss of taste/smell, sore throat, etc.), exposed to positive COVID-19 case within last 14 days, or at high-risk for COVID-19.
  • For any unvaccinated person or those taking extra precautions, masks are encouraged while inside the Dining Hall building until seated at tables and eating. Disposable masks available at entry for those who do not have them.
  • All diners are asked to sanitize their hands before touching serving utensils and surfaces along the buffet. Hand sanitizer dispensers at entry and at start of buffet line.
  • Capacity restrictions have been eliminated in Michigan. However, diners are asked to move outdoors promptly after finishing meal to keep numbers down in the Dining Hall.

General Rules and Expectations

  • All members, guests, and renters are expected to take their meals at the Dining Hall while in camp during the season.
  • Young children should be accompanied and supervised in the Dining Hall by an older, responsible person.
  • No smoking is permitted in the Dining Hall. All cigarette butts must be extinguished and disposed of properly. Members and Guests should refrain from drinking alcohol in the Dining Hall.
